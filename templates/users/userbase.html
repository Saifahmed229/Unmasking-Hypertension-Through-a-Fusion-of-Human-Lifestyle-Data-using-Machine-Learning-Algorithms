Hypertension Projection Using Machine Learning

Leveraging AI for Early Detection and Management of High Blood Pressure

About HyperPredict

HyperPredict is an innovative platform that utilizes Machine Learning algorithms to predict the risk of hypertension. By analyzing various health parameters, we aim to provide early warnings and assist in effective management of high blood pressure, thereby reducing the risk of cardiovascular diseases.

How It Works

  • Data Collection: Gathers comprehensive health data including age, BMI, lifestyle habits, and medical history.
  • Machine Learning Model: Applies advanced machine learning algorithms to predict the risk of hypertension based on the collected data.
  • Risk Evaluation: Provides a personalized risk evaluation and suggests lifestyle improvements or medical intervention if necessary.
